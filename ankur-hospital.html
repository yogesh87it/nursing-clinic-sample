About Us

Trusted Healthcare in
the Heart of Mapusa

Ankur Hospital has been providing professional medical care to families in Mapusa and across North Goa for over two decades. Founded with a commitment to compassionate, evidence-based healthcare, we have grown to become a recognised facility for women's health and maternity services in the region.

Our team of experienced doctors, nurses, and support staff are guided by the ethical standards of the Medical Council of India, ensuring that every patient receives dignified, transparent, and informed care.

What We Offer

Medical Services

Specialist services delivered by qualified professionals in a safe, accredited clinical environment.

🩺
Gynecology

Comprehensive women's health consultations, diagnosis, and treatment across all stages of life.

  • Routine gynaecological checkups
  • PCOS & hormonal disorder management
  • Menstrual irregularity treatment
  • Ovarian cyst evaluation & care
🤱
Obstetrics & Maternity

End-to-end pregnancy care from antenatal consultations through to postnatal support.

  • Antenatal & postnatal care
  • Normal & C-section deliveries
  • High-risk pregnancy monitoring
  • Newborn care & support
🔬
Fertility Consultation

Assessment and counselling for couples navigating fertility concerns, with compassionate guidance.

  • Fertility evaluation & history review
  • Hormonal & diagnostic assessments
  • Ovulation tracking guidance
  • Referral coordination where required
🔦
Laparoscopic Procedures

Minimally invasive surgical procedures for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes.

  • Laparoscopic hysterectomy
  • Ovarian cyst removal
  • Diagnostic laparoscopy
  • Tubal ligation procedures
📡
Ultrasound & Diagnostics

On-site imaging and diagnostic services for accurate, timely clinical assessment.

  • Obstetric & fetal ultrasound
  • Pelvic & abdominal scanning
  • Level II anomaly scanning
  • Colour Doppler studies
💊
General Medicine

OPD consultations for general health concerns, routine checkups, and preventive care.

  • General OPD consultations
  • Preventive health evaluations
  • Chronic condition follow-ups
  • Minor procedure & wound care
Infrastructure

Our Facilities

A well-equipped, hygienic clinical environment designed for patient safety, comfort, and efficient care delivery.

🛏️
Private & Semi-Private Rooms
Comfortable, private rooms for maternity admissions and post-operative recovery with attendant facilities.
🏥
Labour & Delivery Ward
Dedicated labour ward with continuous monitoring equipment and a trained midwifery team.
⚕️
Operation Theatre
Fully equipped OT for laparoscopic, obstetric, and gynaecological surgical procedures.
👶
Nursery & Neonatal Care
Dedicated nursery with warming facilities for newborn observation and neonatal monitoring.
📡
Diagnostic Imaging Unit
On-site ultrasound and diagnostic imaging available for timely, in-house assessments.
💉
Pathology & Laboratory
In-house laboratory for routine blood work, urinalysis, and prenatal diagnostic testing.
🚑
24-Hour Emergency Unit
Emergency care available around the clock for obstetric emergencies and urgent gynaecological conditions.
💊
In-House Pharmacy
On-premise pharmacy for convenient access to prescribed medications and post-procedure supplies.
🅿️
Parking & Accessibility
Adequate parking space with ramp access for patients with mobility needs and ambulance bay access.
